About Share IITK

Share IITK provides the campus junta a platform to share study material online. After this you have to take the trouble of making the pilgrimage to the photocopy shop or waiting for hours to get the assignment or class notes. The content is effectively tagged and the search platform makes it very convenient to procure the study material. No excuses now! Go rock the academics!!
